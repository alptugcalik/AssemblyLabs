NOTICE THAT TIMER 0 IS USED FOR PWM AND PB6 PIN IS USED

PB6 IS CONNECTED TO RED LED BY CABLE(PF1)

SYSTICK INTERRUPT IS USED FOR 1 S DELAY. RELOAD VALUE IS FOUND BY CONSIDERING 1 CLOCK CYCLE OF SYSTCK IS 250ns.

PWM IS GENERATED ON PORT B.

SINCE THE CIRCUIT READS ANALOG DATA AT EVERY 1s, THE BRIGHTNESS CHANGE CAN HAVE A DELAY.

PE3 IS CONNECTED TO THE MIDDLE PORT OF THE POT.

THE READ VOLTAGE VALUES ARE ALSO PRINTED ON TERMITE. YOU CAN CHECK THE VALUES.
